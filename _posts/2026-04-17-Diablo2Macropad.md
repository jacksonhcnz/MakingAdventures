date: 2026-04-17

By default Diable 2 uses 1-4 on the number row for potions and F1-F8 for skills (along with mouse one and mouse two).
On my keyboard its not very easy to get to the skills while also having potions close at hand.

To fix this I built a small 12 key macropad.
It's a handwired "keyboard" running QMK on a Pro Micro RP2040.
The switches are some Cherry Reds I had left over from a Dactyl Manuform.
The pad is a sandwatch lazercut acrylic case with the switches handwired in.
The caddy and palmrest are 3D printed PLA with a heat insert for it to be adjustable.
I ended up replaceing the acrylic base with a printed one that incorperated a holder for the micro controller.
